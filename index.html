Glass Heart Lexicon

A catalog of incented words for the instincts and voids that live beyond ordinary language. With stark ink and painting echoing the weight of its definition this book reveals the horrid beauty hidden in the fractured obsessions, hungers, and cruelties that shape us when no one is watching. The Glass Heart Lexicon is a poetic mixture of dark psychology and art that capture the grotesque and ludicrous workings of the antisocial mind. For readers drawn to the strange, the unsettling and seductively unspoken.
